## 🏛 The "Royal PM" Workflow

The expressions above reference the **Royal We** tradition — a rhetorical form where a single authority speaks as *"we"*.  
In this context, it represents a workflow where one human PM orchestrates a structured team of AI agents.

In practice, this means:

- The PM focuses on **architecture, system boundaries, and technology selection**
- Research AIs investigate algorithms and technical options
- Architecture AIs assist with **interface contracts, documentation, and UML diagrams**
- Business-flow AIs translate product requirements into **executable pipelines**
- Coding AIs implement individual components
- The PM continuously **reviews, supervises, and coordinates** the system

The philosophy is simple:

> One architect, many agents — each fulfilling a clear role.

Like a disciplined bureaucracy, every component has its responsibility,  
and the PM ensures the entire system remains **coherent, auditable, and production-ready**.

I am currently practicing and refining this **"We are the PM" workflow** as a practical approach to AI-assisted software development.
